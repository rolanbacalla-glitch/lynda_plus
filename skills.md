# Lyssna-Plus AI Agentic Skills (SKILLS.md)

This platform relies on a suite of "Agentic Skills" powered by Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash to automate the user research lifecycle. 

## 1. Research Design (The Architect)
- **Bias Detective**: Real-time monitoring of study questions. Flags leading, loaded, or double-barreled questions.
- **Scenario Generator**: Given a project brief, automatically drafts 5 possible research scenarios.
- **Inclusive Auditor**: Scans participant criteria to identify potential data gaps in demographics.

## 2. Moderation (The Co-pilot)
- **Sentiment Real-time HUD**: During live interviews, shows a dashboard of user sentiment (Anxious, Delighted, Frustrated).
- **Auto-follow-up Prompting**: Suggests deeper follow-up questions to the moderator based on unusual user responses.

## 3. Synthesis (The Analyst)
- **Flash-Sync Video Highlights**: Compresses hour-long recordings into thematic "Insight Clips" with 90% accuracy.
- **Thematic Grid Builder**: Automatically categorizes findings into the "Lyssna-Plus" grid (Time-to-Insight, NPS impact, UI friction).
- **Boardroom Report Gen**: Exports findings into high-end PDF/Web decks for stakeholder review.
